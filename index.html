Welcome! This is the website of Daijiang Li (李代江). I am currently a first second-year PhD student in the Department of Botany, University of Wisconsin - Madison, working with Don Waller. I got my Bachelor degree from Yunnan University at 2008, with Ecology as my major. After then, I completed my Master study under the direction of Dr. Shaolin Peng, in School of Life Science, Sun Yat-sen University.

I am interested with "the causes and consequences of biodiversity" in community ecology, one of the central goals in ecology. In the face of rapid environmental change worldwide, understanding community assemble rules, community dynamics, the forces and processes underlying and the relationships between biodiversity and ecosystem functioning will be crucial for conserving, managing and restoring the overall biodiversity.
